The Old Curiosity Shop


  The Old Curiosity Shop is one of two novels (the other being Barnaby Rudge) which Charles Dickens published along
with short stories in his weekly serial Master Humphrey's Clock, from 1840 to 1841. It was so popular that New York readers
stormed the wharf when the ship bearing the final instalment arrived in 1841. The Old Curiosity Shop was printed in book form
in 1841. The plot follows the life of Nell Trent and her grandfather, both residents of The Old Curiosity Shop in London.
